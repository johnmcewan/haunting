Welcome to Haunted Lawrence, a digital project by the 2025 Institute of Digital Research Scholars at KU University, that explores the rich paranormal history of Lawrence, Kansas. Founded on the belief that every ghost has a story to tell, our project serves as a bridge between the living and the departed, ensuring that no spirit is forgotten.

Our interactive map reveals the locations where the veil between worlds grows thin, where echoes of the past continue to resonate in the present.

‚ö∞Ô∏è Why Lawrence is Haunted

1854

Bleeding Kansas

Founded during the violent Bleeding Kansas period, Lawrence was born from conflict and bloodshed, setting the stage for restless spirits.

1863

Quantrill's Raid

William Quantrill's Confederate raiders burned much of Lawrence, killing over 150 men and boys. The trauma of this massacre left deep spiritual scars.

1866

University Founded

The University of Kansas was established, bringing centuries of accumulated knowledge, experiments, and young lives to the area.

Present

Modern Hauntings

Today, Lawrence's rich history continues to manifest in paranormal activity, with new encounters reported regularly throughout the city.
